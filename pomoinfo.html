Background Information

The Pomodoro Technique

This working timer is based on the Pomodoro time management technique.

The basic idea is to break down your task into chunks of working intervalls of 25 minutes (one pomodoro) each. Between the pomodori, take a short break for 5 minutes. After four pomodori, take a longer break of about 15 - 20 minutes.

Within each pomodoro, try to stay focused and to ignore all kinds of distraction. If you feel the urge to look into your emails or to google something --- jot it down to make sure you do not forget about it, and wait for the break to do all these things. Or take a bath instead!

Working with a timer can thus help you stay focused.

Navigation

After you started a timer, you can stop the timer and return to the starting screen by pressing the escape-key (or touch the screen).
